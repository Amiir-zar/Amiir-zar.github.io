DataCamp Data Scientist with Python


This program consists of 23 courses, with each course focusing on a specific topic, such as data cleaning, data visualization, and statistical modeling



What I have Learned:

After months of hard work and dedication, I am proud to say that I have successfully completed the DataCamp Data Scientist Track. I spent more than 100 hours on the program, completing 25 Courses, 6 Projects, and 3 Skill Assessments.

Throughout the program, I gained a deep understanding of the core concepts and techniques used in data science, including data cleaning and manipulation, exploratory data analysis, statistical inference, machine learning, and data visualization. I obtained a comprehensive education, covering everything from Python programming basics to advanced subjects like supervised and unsupervised machine learning.

One of the things I appreciated most about the program was its emphasis on practical applications. Each course included several exercises and projects designed to give me hands-on experience applying the concepts and techniques I had learned. I worked with real datasets, including data from the music streaming service Spotify, the video game platform Steam, and the stock market.

Overall, completing the Data Scientist Track was a long and challenging road, but it was also an incredibly rewarding one. I am now equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to tackle complex data problems and make data-driven decisions. I look forward to applying what I have learned in my future endeavors and would highly recommend the DataCamp Data Scientist track to anyone looking to start or advance their career in data science.
